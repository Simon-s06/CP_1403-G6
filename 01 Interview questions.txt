SDG8: Decent Work
1. Walk me through the last time you had to work overtime nonstop to keep your project on track.
2. What difficulties did you run into while sorting out employment terms when building your six-person startup team?
3. Could you outline your typical workday tasks related to staff payroll and work scheduling?
4. How do you normally respond when your team members bring up concerns about work benefits and conditions?
5. Tell me what you did the last time tight cash flow forced you to cut manpower costs for the business.
6. Which details make you struggle between hiring expenses and local employment rules during recruitment?
7. How do you adjust arrangements when employees struggle to keep up with their workload?
8. Aside from base salaries, what unexpected monthly costs do you cover for your team’s benefits?
9. What follow-up issues popped up after you cut your team’s downtime to rush a tight deadline last time?
10. How would you describe hiring and managing startup staff to other local founders?
