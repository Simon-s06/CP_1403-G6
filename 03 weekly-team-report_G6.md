# Jia Hui Persona Analysis

## Needs

| Needs                                               | Interview Evidence                                                                                 |
| --------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Wants to keep good employees with a small budget    | She cannot give high salaries like big companies, but she wants employees to feel safe and valued. |
| Needs easier HR tools                               | She often manages attendance, schedules, leave records, and payroll.                               |
| Needs better ways to share work                     | When employees have too much work, she changes deadlines and gives tasks to others.                |
| Wants open communication with her team              | She listens to employees and tells them honestly what the company can and cannot do.               |
| Wants employees to stay healthy                     | She does not want employees to work too much and become too tired.                                 |
| Needs stable cash flow                              | She wants enough money to avoid stopping hiring or reducing staff.                                 |
| Needs to follow employment rules while saving costs | She needs to think about EPF, SOCSO, paid leave, and other legal duties.                           |
| Wants less stress                                   | She feels anxious but tries to stay calm for her team.                                             |

---

## Pain Points

| Pain Points                          | Evidence from the Interview                                              |
| ------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Long working hours make people tired | She worked until after midnight for four nights before an investor demo. |
| Small hiring budget                  | The company cannot offer salaries like larger companies.                 |
| Staff costs are higher than expected | Salaries, EPF, SOCSO, paid leave, and benefits all cost money.           |
| Cash flow problems                   | Late payments from clients can affect salary payments.                   |
| Not enough staff                     | Existing employees must do extra work when hiring is frozen.             |
| Employee burnout                     | The team needed one or two weeks to recover after overtime.              |
| Lower work quality                   | Being tired caused more mistakes and lower performance.                  |
| More team conflicts                  | Small problems became bigger arguments during stressful times.           |
| Too many responsibilities            | She works as a founder, HR manager, mediator, and payroll manager.       |
| Uncertain decisions                  | She often makes decisions without having all the information.            |
| Hidden stress                        | She hides her anxiety and acts confident in front of the team.           |

---

## Core Problem Statement

> **As a small startup founder, Jia Hui needs to take care of her employees, follow employment rules, and manage limited money, while making sure the business can continue to run well.**

**Chinese Translation:**

> 作为一家小型创业公司的创始人，Jia Hui 需要照顾员工、遵守劳动法规，并管理有限的资金，同时确保公司能够持续稳定地运营。

## User Requirements

| User Requirements                                                                    |
| ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| The system should help founders check employee workloads and share tasks fairly.     |
| The system should calculate payroll automatically, including EPF and SOCSO payments. |
| The system should record leave, attendance, and work schedules in one place.         |
| The system should warn managers when employees work too much overtime.               |
| The system should show staff costs and cash flow clearly.                            |
| The system should support open communication about benefits and company policies.    |
| The system should create reports to help with hiring and staffing decisions.         |

---

## Summary

### Jia Hui's Core Needs

* Manage the team without making employees too tired.
* Control staff costs with a limited budget.
* Make HR work easier and faster.
* Reduce stress and pressure when making decisions.

### Jia Hui's Biggest Challenges

* **Not enough money (cash flow)**
* **Not enough people (manpower)**
* **Not enough time (overtime)**
* **Too much stress (anxiety)**

These problems are connected and are the main challenges Jia Hui faces as a small startup founder.
